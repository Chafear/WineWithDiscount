Description

Minimalstic keyboard-based arcade game. In SURVIVORS LEFT: X you make 50 survivors to pass through dangerous puzzle levels. Quick tip: DON`T mess with Crabo.

Features

  • Kill your time - dont kill survivors.
  • Make you "feel" like a person pressing buttions on a keyboard.
  • Challenging but not overstressed.
  • Minimalistic
  • Pixel-art
  • Perfect for few chill sessions, nothing more B)
